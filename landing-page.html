Purpose

Lotus was created with the intent of raising awareness on mental health, which is deemed a colossal issue among people of all ages from all around the world. Moreover, it was made to encourage habits of self care such as getting a sufficient amount of sleep, water, and meals in a day, which assist in maintaining one's mental health.
Regarding our website's name, it was named after a flower that symbolizes strength, resilience, and rebirth. The Lotus plant begins its journey rooted deep within layers of soil, water, and mud. Instead of being defeated by its desolate surroundings, it uses its resources to pave way for growth, and rises up to the surface before finally blossoming into a beautiful flower.
Just like the Lotus, we believe that people who find themselves rooted within layers of stress, trouble, and pressure can grow into the best versions of themselves and rise up to the surface before blossoming into happy, and healthy individuals.

Be Happy, Be Healthy, Be Lotus
