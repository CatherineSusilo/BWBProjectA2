About Us

We, team A2, joined the Buddies without Borders Forum called the Happy Teens Website Design Challenge. Our team consists of Alessandra Corine Torregoza Silapan, Catherine Susilo, Michelle Audrey Anggito, and Sukrit Birmani who have worked hard to make this website. We aim to reduce the stigma on mental health and help those who are struggling with them.
